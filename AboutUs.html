About Us

MCFam is a Brokerage Real Estate Company

Company Profile

MCFam Realty offers a complete portfolio of real estate services, including sales and leasing of

commercial and residential property, investment sales and acquisitions, project sales and marketing,

property consultancy, mortgage services, interior decoration & design, and relocation services. We

conduct research studies and valuation surveys upon request. With its humble beginning being a

partner-marketing arm of Olryt Marketing and Development Inc., MCFam Realty ventured on its own

last February of 2019 to continuously deliver its impeccable services to its clients. Imbued and driven

with its mission and vision of creating outstanding communities that enrich the lives and values of the

families, we guide and give advises to individuals, families and corporates on growth strategies and

real estate investment opportunities in current and new markets. MCFam Realty has a professional

and committed team of sales and leasing property advisors, all of whom are PRC registered and

certified brokers and sellers.


Michael C. Canino, CEO and founder of MCFam Realty,opened the doors of his real estate company

in 2019. Bringing with him over 16 years’ experience in the real estate industry being a broker partner

of top developers like Ayala Land Inc., DMCI Homes, SM Prime Holdings Inc., Megaworld Corporation,

Filinvest Land Inc., Vista Land and Lifescapes Inc., Robinsons Land Corporation, Federal Land Inc.,

Shang Properties Inc., Century Properties Group Inc., Anchor Land Holdings Inc., Cathayland Inc.,

and others. With an extensive portfolio of accomplishments, he has focused his energy, experience

and expertise into his firm, through which he offers an extensive portfolio of real estate services.


To extend the company’s position as trusted real estate professionals in the Philippines, we intend to

grow the market for MCFam Realty through a complete range of real estate services, provided by an

energetic team of real estate sales people and consultants. Our mission at MCFam Realty is to provide

individuals, families, businesses, investors and developers with a complete solution to real estate with

exceptional and personalized service in all their property needs.
